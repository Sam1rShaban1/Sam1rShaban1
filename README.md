### The Developer Journey

Hi, I'm **Samir**. Based in **North Macedonia**, I am a Computer Science Senior at **SEEU** and a **Research Intern** at the Max van Der Stoel Institute.

My journey started in **hardware design** (working as a CAD Designer & CNC Programmer), giving me a unique perspective on the physical world. Today, I combine that engineering mindset with **Deep Learning** to solve complex environmental problems.

I don't just train models; I deploy them to the **Edge**. My work focuses on creating **Hybrid AI Systems** that run on constrained devices like Raspberry Pis and ESP32s to monitor air quality and health metrics in real-time.

### Featured Research & Implementations

<details>
<summary><b>1. Decentralized Air Quality Forecasting (Click to Expand)</b></summary>
<br>
<blockquote>
  A complex Deep Learning system designed to predict PM1, PM2.5, and PM10 pollutants.
</blockquote>

*   **The Challenge:** Air quality data is often siloed and predicting localized pollution is difficult with general models.
*   **The Solution:** I developed a **Hybrid WOA-CNN-BiLSTM-AM** model.
    *   **WOA:** Whale Optimization Algorithm for hyperparameter tuning.
    *   **CNN:** Extracts spatial features from sensor grids.
    *   **BiLSTM:** Captures temporal dependencies in time-series data.
    *   **Federated Learning:** Allows local models to train on edge devices without sending raw data to the cloud, preserving privacy.
*   **Tech Stack:** `Python` `TensorFlow` `Federated Learning` `Matplotlib`
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>2. Chest X-ray Disease Classification (Click to Expand)</b></summary>
<br>
<blockquote>
  Medical imaging AI using State-of-the-Art Vision Transformers.
</blockquote>

*   **The Challenge:** Accurately identifying multiple diseases in a single X-ray image.
*   **The Solution:** Fine-tuned a **Vision Transformer (ViT)** on the massive NIH ChestXray14 dataset.
*   **Performance:** Utilized **Google Cloud TPUs** for high-speed training and implemented **Grad-CAM** to visualize exactly which part of the lung the AI was looking at to make a diagnosis.
*   **Tech Stack:** `PyTorch` `Hugging Face Transformers` `GCP`
</details>

<details>
<summary><b>3. Long-Range IoT Edge Network (Click to Expand)</b></summary>
<br>
<blockquote>
  A physical hardware network for collecting environmental data off-grid.
</blockquote>

*   **Hardware:** Arduino Nano 33 BLE (Sensing) + LilyGO T-Beam (Transmission).
*   **Logic:** The Nano collects Temperature/Humidity/Pressure and sends it via BLE to the T-Beam, which transmits data kilometers away using **LoRa** technology.
*   **Optimization:** Implemented deep sleep cycles to run on battery power for extended periods.
*   **Tech Stack:** `C++` `Arduino IDE` `LoRa` `Meshtastic`
</details>
